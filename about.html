Our Beginning

Founded in 2020, EBS Ecommerce started with a simple vision: to make quality products accessible to everyone, everywhere. What began as a small startup with just 5 team members has grown into one of India's most trusted online shopping platforms, serving millions of customers across the country. Our journey is a testament to the trust our customers place in us.
